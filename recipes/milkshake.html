Peanut Butter Milkshake

Description

This simple milkshake has been a longtime favorite of mine. It is the perfect antidote to a bad day, or a great way to make your afternoon feel special.

Ingredients

  • Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Whole Milk
  • Peanut Butter

Equipment

  • Immersion Blender

Directions

  1. Add a scoop of ice cream to the cup of the immersion blender. Add about half a cup of milk and blend until smooth.
  2. Using a spoon, check the consistency of your milkshake. Add more milk to make it thinner, or more ice cream for a thicker consistency.
  3. Add 2-3 large tablespoons of peanut butter and blend.
  4. For a delicious Peanut Butter Cup flavor, substitute cholocate ice cream for the vanilla.
