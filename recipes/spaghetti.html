Spaghetti

Description

This easy, no-fuss spaghetti is a weeknight staple at our house. It goes together in minutes, makes for a hearty meal, and never disappoints. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 tbl Olive Oil
  • 1 lb Mushrooms, cleaned and sliced
  • 32 oz jar Rao's "Tomato and Basil" sauce
  • 1 lb Spaghetti
  • Grated Parmesan
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Bring a large part of salted water to boil, and cook spaghetti according to package directions.
  2. Meanwhile, brown the ground beef in a large skillet.
  3. If the pan is dry, add a tbl of olive oil.
  4. Add mushrooms, and saute several minutes until mushrooms are nicely browned and tender.
  5. While the mushrooms are browing, add a teaspoon of salt and a teaspoon of black pepper. Stir to combine well.
  6. If necessary, drain extra liquid from the beef and mushroom mixture. Add the jar of sauce and stir to combine well.
  7. Let the sauce come to a simmer. Scrape any browned bits from the bottom of the pan.
  8. When the pasta is done, drain it well, and combine with the sauce.
  9. Serve and top with freshly grated parmesan to taste.
