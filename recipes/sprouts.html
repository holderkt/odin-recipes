Roasted Brussels Sprouts

Description

When I met my husband, he was not a big fan of vegetables, to say the least. Over the years, I have learned that cooking veggies well makes all the difference. These quick-roasted brussels sprouts are now one of his favorite foods. He'll eat pounds at a time!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Brussels Sprouts
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Crushed Red Pepper (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
  2. Slice the stem end off each Brussels Sprout, and then slice each one in half. If any sprouts are significantly larger than the others, cut those into quarters.
  3. Pile the Brussels Sprouts onto a cookie sheet and add several tablespoons of olive oil.
  4. Toss the sprouts until they have a thin coating of oil. Add more olive oil if needed.
  5. Spread the sprouts out on the cookie sheet, cut sides down.
  6. Sprinkle the sprouts generously with salt and pepper. For a hint of spice, add a sprinkle of crushed red pepper.
  7. Roast sprouts until they are darkly browned and the cut sides are crispy (approximately 30 minutes). It is hard to really overcook these. They taste great with some char!
  8. Taste the sprouts and add seasoning if necessary. Don't be shy about adding more salt!
